How it Works

  1. Diagnose: Chat with our AI to clearly define your challenge.
  2. Search: Our agents dynamically generate queries and scrape professional networks.
  3. Evaluate: We run candidates' resumes against your specific problem using an LLM to generate a SolverScore.
  4. Match: Review the top candidates and compare strengths.

About SolverSearch

SolverSearch is an AI-native expert matching platform. Instead of searching by job titles or generic keywords, you describe the exact business or technical problem you are facing.

Our autonomous agents crawl, scrape, and evaluate thousands of professional profiles to find the exact individuals who have provably solved your specific problem in the past.

FAQ

How is SolverScore calculated?

SolverScore is generated by our custom LLM pipeline evaluating the parsed resume against your specific problem diagnosis in real-time.

Do you bypass LinkedIn's anti-scraping?

We utilize enterprise-grade residential proxy APIs to gather publicly available data safely and reliably.
