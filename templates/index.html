How it Works

  1. Diagnose: Chat with our AI to clearly define your challenge.
  2. Classify: Our agent decides which solutions fit — experts, tools, agencies, or a combination.
  3. Search: Parallel agents scour LinkedIn, ProductHunt/G2, and Clutch/GoodFirms.
  4. Evaluate: An LLM scores every candidate against your specific problem.
  5. Synthesize: Review your recommended Solution Stack and compare options.
  6. Refine: Keep chatting to refine the diagnosis — every new message triggers a fresh, updated search.

About SolverSearch

SolverSearch is an AI-native problem-to-solution matchmaker. Instead of searching by job titles or generic keywords, you describe the exact business or technical problem you are facing.

Our autonomous agents crawl, scrape, and evaluate human experts, software tools, and consulting firms in parallel — then synthesize a recommended Solution Stack for your specific situation.

FAQ

How is SolverScore calculated?

SolverScore is generated by our custom LLM pipeline evaluating each candidate (expert, tool, or agency) against your specific problem diagnosis in real-time.

How are data sources collected?

We utilize enterprise-grade scraping APIs to gather publicly available data from professional networks and software directories safely and reliably.

What does refining a search do?

Every refinement is a fresh search. The AI takes your full conversation into account, produces an updated diagnosis, and runs a new search from scratch — replacing all previous results.
